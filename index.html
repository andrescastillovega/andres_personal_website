Hello!✌️

I am a Toronto-based data scientist and a graduate student at the University of Toronto. I am working as a research assistant at the Travel Modelling Group - TMG under the supervision of Prof. Eric Miller. Before grad school, I worked as a Senior Consultant at Steer, an international consulting firm focused on the transportation sector.

My current interests include machine learning and probabilistic programming to solve complex problems such as urban congestion. I am convinced that technology is a powerful tool to improve the quality of life in the cities, so I am passionate about using my skills to make a positive impact in the world.

I am currently learning about Open-Source LLMs, the Transformer architecture, and the JAX library.

Besides work, I like to spend time reading, walking my dog, and learning new things (including playing tennis). Also, a big fan of maps and typography.
