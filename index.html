Hello!✌️

I am a urban data scientist and a recent graduate from the University of Toronto. I am actively seeking opportunities in roles such as data scientist, machine learning engineer, and transport modeller.

During my graduate studies, I worked as a research assistant at the Travel Modelling Group - TMG mentored by Prof. Eric Miller. Prior my graduate studies, I worked as a Senior Consultant at Steer, where I applied machine learning algorithms in the transport modelling practice.

My current research interest includes the application of machine learning and probabilistic programming to address urban challenges such as congestion. I strongly believe in the power of technology to enhance urban life, so I enjoy using my skills to contribute positively to tackle this issues.

I am currently learning about Open-Source LLMs, the Transformer architecture, and the JAX library.

Besides work, I also like to spend time reading, walking my dog, playing tennis and learning new things. Also, I am a big fan of maps and typography.
